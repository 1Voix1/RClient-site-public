What's inside the client?

Find/Copy Skins

Conveniently search and copy skins directly in the game. Find any skin by player nickname.

Enhanced Lasers

Customize laser color and thickness for better visibility, just like in Pulse Client.

Auto Clan Change

The client can automatically change your clan depending on the server you are playing on.

Ingame Scoreboard Menu

Interact with players directly through the scoreboard using a convenient context menu.

Custom HUD

Customize your Heads-Up Display to show the information you care about most.

Ghost Mode

Practice maps by racing against your own best times with a visible ghost tee.

Advanced Chat

Enjoy an improved chat with message history, tabs, and nickname highlighting.

Performance Boost

Optimized code for smoother gameplay and higher frames-per-second.
