RClient for DDNet

Experience the next level of DDRaceNetwork. Based on Tater Client with the best features from Pulse and Entity clients.

Download Discord

Base: Tater Client

RClient is built on the Tater Client, using its functions and many others as a foundation.

Inspired by: Pulse Client

We have taken and improved the most useful features from Pulse, such as laser customization and the scoreboard menu.

Enhanced with: Entity Client

We integrated useful QoL features from Entity to make your gaming experience even more comfortable.

Features

Search info

Search and display player or map data from DDStats

Tracker

Show the positions of other players that you can add to the tracker.

Censor list

Censor and replace unwanted words everywhere chat, nameplates, scoreboard and etc.

Copy player

Clone player or skins from your server or DDStats. Do not use it for bad purposes!

Nameplates

Customize the nameplates as you wish using a simple nameplate editor.

Chat bubbles

See the messages of the players above their heads.

Scoreboard (Pulse)

Upgraded scoreboard that allows you to perform quick actions.

And more

There are many functions that don't fit here. Explore even more features in the game.
